About My Pencil Art

Anandhan is a pencil artist who creates detailed sketches of portraits, nature, and everyday life, using only graphite and paper.

My Story

Pencil drawing started as a simple hobby and slowly turned into a serious passion. Over the years, constant practice and observation have helped shape a realistic, detail-focused style.

Each artwork is built layer by layer, using light strokes to capture emotions, textures, and subtle shadows that bring a drawing to life.

What I Draw

  • Realistic portrait sketches
  • Nature and wildlife pencil drawings
  • Custom commission artworks from your photos

Commissions & Collaboration

Custom pencil sketches can be created for birthdays, anniversaries, gifts, or personal collections. Work is available in different sizes and formats, from A5 to A3 and above.
